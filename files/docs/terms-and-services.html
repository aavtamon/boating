This web site uses Cookies to improve user experience and allow to create and modify reservations that you place using this web site.
We collect your personal information and store it on our server until your ride is complete. Soon after it completes, we remove all your personal information. While it is not likely, it is still possible that our computer systems can be hacked into, and the personal infirmation you provided can be stolen. By accepting this policy you understand and accept this risks. In order to lower the risk, we do not store this information for longer than it is needed to complete processing of your rental. We keep information about the DL and safety test results for 180 days. While your DL number will be stored for 180 days, information about your name and other personal information will be removed sooner.
We do not save your credit card information. Instead, we securily transfer it to the payment service Stripe.
