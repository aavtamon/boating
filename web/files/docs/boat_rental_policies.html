Boat Rental Policies

·      Renter

o   The renter must be at least 25 years of age.

o   The renter must have a valid, unexpired state issued driver’s license. No other form of ID will be accepted.  We will need to make a copy of the driver's license on the day of the rental.

o   The name on the renter's credit card, must match the name on the driver’s license.

o   The renter of the boat, and the operator of the boat  (the driver) need not be the same individual.  

·      Boat Operator

o   Any/all operators (drivers) of the boat must watch a 10 minute DNR boating safety video, and pass an on-line safety test.  

o   Any/all operators (drivers) of the boat must have a valid unexpired state issued driver’s license on their person at all times.

o   No one under the age of 16 can operate (drive) the boat (state law).

·      Safety

o   The number of passengers permitted on a boat, is determined by the manufacture, and not the number of seats on the boat. The capacity of the boat cannot be exceeded.

o   Life jackets will be provided for all individuals, however we do NOT provide life jackets for infants.  Infant life jackets are the responsibility of the renter.

o   Anyone under the age of 13 MUST wear a life jacket at all times (state law). Parents are responsible for ensuring their children are wearing a life jacket of the correct size.

o   We strongly suggest that any non-swimmer who is over the age of 13 wear a life jacket at all times.

o   Anyone who in swimming/tubing/skiing on the lake must wear a life jacket at all times.

o   When the boat is in motion (or the engine running), no one can sit outside the rail

·      Pre and Post Trip Inspection

o   Prior to the beginning of the rental period the renter will be walked thru all operational and safety aspects of the boat.   This includes but is not limited to starting/operating the boat, locations of life jackets, anchors, fire extinguisher and other equipment.

o   The boat hull, motor, upholstery and other equipment. will be examined for damage prior to the start of the rental period.

o   Following the end of the rental period, the boat will be re-examined for damage.

o   The renter is responsible for any damage to the boat.

·      Fuel

o   The boat will be furnished to you with a full tank of fuel.

o   The renter will be charged for the fuel consumed during the course of the rental. 

o   There are number of refueling docks around the lake (located in the various marinas).  Fuel prices vary, but are generally $1 to $2 above the standard price you would pay at your local gas station.

o   The refueling charge will be based on the average gas station price per gallon. 

·      Deposit

o   In addition to rental fees, a $500 security deposit is required and will be charged to the renters credit card when rental begins.  We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.  We do not accept debit cards, gift cards, cash or checks.

o   The security deposit will be refunded back to the original credit card following the final inspection, less any refueling charge.

·      Late Fees

o   The boat must be returned promptly at the end of the rental period and brought back to the location it was picked up from.   Late returns will be charged $25.00 for every 15 minutes the boat is late.    If you know you are going to be late, please contact Pizboats regarding an expected arrival time.

·      Misc.

o   Use of the boat is limited to daylight hours.

o   All passengers will be required to sign a Release of Liability. Parents must sign the release for anyone under the age of 18.

o   A cancellation with less than 4 hour notice, or a "NO SHOW" will forfeit entire reservation fee and cannot be applied toward a future reservation.

o   Cancellations fees cannot be waived for personal emergencies

o   Cancellation fees will only be waived if inclement weather conditions exist on the lake at the time of your rental. Slight rain or cloudy weather are not justifications for cancellation or rescheduling.

o   If weather conditions change during your rental, we will "Pro-Rate" your rental fee based on the amount of time spent on the lake.

o   We may elect to cancel your reservation if weather conditions justify the need.

o   We may cancel your reservation in advance if any unforeseen circumstance should arise.

o   No pets are allowed.

o   No cooking (grilling) on the boat is allowed.

o   The renter must remove all trash from the boat.  There is a $40 charge for cleaning the boat (excessive mud, trash, etc.).
