Rental Management Documents
Document Description
Renter's Agreement Rental contract. Two copies must be printed and signed. One goes to the renter, the other one is kept by PizBoats.
Renter's Waiver One copy is filled and signed by the renter and is kept by PizBoats.
Boat Inspection Form One copy is filled and signed by the renter and is kept by PizBoats.
Rental Policies The form is printed and provided to the renter.
Boat Owner Documents
Document Description
Boat Owner Agreement Boat Owner Contract. Two copies must be printed and signed. One copy goes to the boat owner. The other is kept by PizBoats.
Personal Boat Usage Policy The policy is provided to the boat owner as a summary of his boat usage rights and responsibilities.
